
Vim-indent-guides
--------------------------------
▶ Indent levels, prakticky bez konfigurace
  Taby, mezery, volba barev
▶ Stažení
    git clone git://github.com/nathanaelkane/vim-indent-guides.git
▶ Po instalaci do ~/.vim/bundle 
    :IndentGuidesEnable
    :IndentGuidesDisable
    :IndentGuidesToggle
    :let g:indent_guides_indent_levels = 30
